Your child's Leadership Journey

Progress through the ranks and take on increasing responsibility

Fourth Form
Foundation Year

Experience CCF, learn basic military skills, drill, and experience all three service sections before choosing specialisation.

Fifth Form
Skills Development

Specialise in chosen service, attend camps and courses, begin taking on junior leadership roles and mentoring new cadets.

Sixth Form
Senior Leadership

Take command as NCOs and senior cadets, lead training, organise activities, and represent the CCF at ceremonial events.
