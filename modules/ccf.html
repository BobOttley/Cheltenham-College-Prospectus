Choose Your Service

After experiencing all three services in Fourth Form, your child will choose their specialisation and embark on a journey of discovery, challenge, and achievement.

Army Section

Develop fieldcraft, tactical skills, and team leadership

  • Duke of Edinburgh integration
  • Annual camps and exercises
  • Weapons training and marksmanship

RAF Section

Experience aviation, air power, and aerospace technology

  • Flying scholarships available
  • RAF base visits and camps
  • Gliding opportunities
